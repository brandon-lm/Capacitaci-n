History

Itachi was born into the Uchiha clan, being the eldest son of the clan leader, Fugaku Uchiha and his wife Mikoto Uchiha. He was always considered and praised as the best in his generation, a genius above all others. He even surpassed the expectations of a skilled member of the Uchiha Clan. However, not even his family, friends or teachers could understand him. When he was only four years old, Itachi had already witnessed the loss of many lives due to the Third Shinobi World War, which emotionally traumatized him, to the point of considering suicide, and made him become a pacifist. At the age of 7, he graduated from the Ninja Academy at the top of his class after only one year, around that time he began to take an interest in the origin of the village, mastered the Sharingan at the age of eight, passed the Chūnin exams at the age of 10, and became a captain of the ANBU units at 13 years old.

He was highly praised by his father, who saw the future of the family in Itachi, so he neglected his youngest son, Sasuke Uchiha. However, during this time Itachi showed great affection towards his younger brother, and even encouraged his father to come to Sasuke's Enrollment ceremony to the Academy, something his father had forgotten saying he had an important mission to establish Itachi to the ANBU ranks.

When the Uchiha Clan began planning to overthrow Konoha, Itachi, as ANBU, was tasked with spying on the village. Knowing that a coup would only lead to a new ninja war, Itachi began spying on the Uchiha instead, relaying information to the Third Hokage and the other elders of Konoha. During this time he began to act strangely, as he would not attend clan meetings, speaking out against the clan and was also considered the prime suspect for the murder of his best friend, Shisui Uchiha, who had drowned in the Nakano River. Because of this, the clan had given up hope on Itachi, and his father began to shift his focus to Sasuke instead. Meanwhile, the Third Hokage's attempts to negotiate a peaceful end to the hostilities between the two sides are proving ineffective; thus, against the Third's wishes, Danzo Shimura and the Konoha Council ordered Itachi to exterminate the entire Uchiha Clan. Itachi agreed on the sole condition that his brother not be harmed, justifying that it must be an Uchiha to execute him in the future for the genocide of the Clan.

Abilities

Itachi Uchiha has demonstrated extraordinary qualities from an early age, such as his intellect and exceptional talent for all ninja disciplines. He managed to graduate from the ninja academy at the young age of 7, become a Chūnin at the age of 10 and captain of an ANBU squad at the age of 13; he was also admired by teachers who assured that his progress did not need any help, it is difficult to name all his achievements, but it cannot be said that it measures Itachi's true ability, his true potential can only be understood by those who come close to his level.

Being a pacifist, due to his traumatic experiences, Itachi does not actively participate in battle, usually only acting in self-defense and trying to defeat his enemies rather than kill them if possible. It could also be said that in each of his battles, Itachi's full strength is kept hidden, either due to his non-violent nature or his terminal illness. However, the times he has been seen fighting he has shown remarkable skills,

    Personality

    Since his first appearance, Itachi had always been a mysterious person and had the intention of hiding his true self. While in his past he proved to be a compassionate brother and a great person, at a certain point in history he had to commit some crimes, even murdering his loved ones and his clan, leaving only one survivor: his younger brother, Sasuke Uchiha. Despite all the shock of his actions, incredibly, he turned out to be that compassionate brother of great courage, making it clear throughout the story that his stay in Akatsuki was also intended to protect his little brother, Sasuke and his village.

    In addition to being an incredibly powerful ninja, Itachi had an equally impressive intelligence, and has proven to be very wise, even Tobi recognized it. He was very strong and attentive to any situation that happened, as he was rarely surprised. He also showed great intuition, as he was almost never fooled and yet almost instantly realized the truth. This intelligence can also be seen through Itachi's immense foresight, as examples, such as foreknowledge of Sasuke's defeat of Deidara and also that his brother survived, his own premeditated fatalistic view of death by Sasuke's hand, and more recently the implantation of his own Kotoamatsukami into the crow within Naruto were just a few examples seen of things that surprised him. Despite his status as a wanted criminal, and ending his clan, Itachi does not enjoy violence or combat, preferring to avoid battle or, if that is not possible, to end it as soon as he can.
